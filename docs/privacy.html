Privacy Policy

Sakina — Sleep to the Quran · Last updated: July 15, 2026

Sakina ("the app", "we") is a Quran recitation, dhikr, and prayer-habit app. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

What we collect

We do not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, contacts, or location.

  • Your preferences and progress (onboarding answers, chosen reciter and surah, reminder times, streaks) are stored only on your device. They are not uploaded to us.
  • Purchase information. Subscriptions are processed by Apple. Our billing provider, RevenueCat, receives an anonymous randomly-generated app user ID and your App Store receipt so your subscription can be validated and restored. This identifier cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Audio streaming. Quran recitations are streamed over the internet from recitation content servers. Those requests include your IP address, as any internet request does. We do not log or store it.

Notifications

Prayer and session reminders are scheduled locally on your device. You can disable them at any time in iOS Settings.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your personal data.
  • We do not show third-party ads or use advertising trackers.
  • We do not access your microphone, camera, contacts, or location.

Subscriptions

Sakina Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple ID. Manage or cancel it any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Payment terms are governed by Apple's standard End User License Agreement.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Data deletion

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app deletes your data. To disconnect the anonymous purchase identifier, contact us and we will ask RevenueCat to delete it.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new "last updated" date.
